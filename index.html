关于我About

我从 UI 设计入行,一路做到产品经理。十几年里做过淘宝联盟的导购广告产品(在阿里妈妈任 P6),负责过海外棋牌平台的增长与赛事运营,也曾从 0 到 1 创办过一家 AI 内容矩阵公司。

I started in UI design and worked my way up to product management. Over the past decade I've built guided-shopping ad products for the Taobao Alliance (as a P6 at Alimama), led growth and tournament operations for an overseas card-game platform, and founded an AI content-matrix company from the ground up.

这些经历让我养成一个习惯:拿到一个含糊的目标,先把它拆成用户路径、前后台需求、运营流程、验收标准和数据复盘机制——让团队照着能做、能测、能复盘,而不是停在一份漂亮但没人能落地的原型上。

That gave me a habit: when I get a fuzzy goal, I break it into user journeys, front- and back-end requirements, operational workflows, acceptance criteria and data-review loops — so the team can actually build, test and learn from it, instead of stopping at a pretty prototype no one can ship.

最近两年我把精力放在两件事:一是用 AI(Codex / ChatGPT / Claude)把调研、需求、原型说明、验收清单这些重复工作产品化、模板化、自动化;二是海外棋牌的赛事活动与 H5/APP 增长体验。我相信好产品经理的价值,不在于会用多少工具,而在于能不能把复杂的事情讲清楚、推下去。

Lately I've focused on two things: using AI (Codex / ChatGPT / Claude) to productize and automate the repetitive work of research, requirements, prototype specs and acceptance checklists; and the tournament activities and H5/APP growth experience of overseas card games. A good PM's value isn't the number of tools they use — it's whether they can make complex things clear and move them forward.
